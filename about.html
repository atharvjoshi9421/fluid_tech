Who we are

Specialists in fluid measurement & handling.

Fluid Tech, headquartered in Shivane, Pune, is a GST-verified manufacturer of industrial flow meters, fuel dispensers, oil filling machines and decanter/centrifuge systems. Over 5+ years we have built a reputation for engineering robust, accurate equipment for some of the most demanding process environments in India.

We work with engineers, plant managers and OEMs across oil & gas, chemical, pharmaceutical, automotive, food and water-treatment sectors — designing equipment around real-world flow ranges, fluid properties and duty cycles.

Manufacturing & Quality

Built, tested and calibrated in-house.

Every Fluid Tech product passes through structured assembly, calibration and load-testing before it leaves our Pune facility. Materials are selected for the specific media — stainless steel, anodised aluminium, PTFE seals — and our PD flow meters are calibrated against verified reference standards.

We back every installation with documentation, training and on-site service so plant teams can run with confidence from day one.
