Who We Are

Pune's Premier Process & Fluid Equipment Specialist

Fluid-Tech, based in Pune, Maharashtra, is a leading supplier and system integrator of fluid handling and process automation equipment. We serve industries including pharmaceuticals, chemicals, food & beverage, water treatment, oil & gas, and manufacturing.

Our comprehensive product portfolio spans centrifugal pumps, gear pumps, diaphragm pumps, ball valves, butterfly valves, control valves, flow meters, level instruments, pressure transmitters, and complete PLC/SCADA automation systems. We are authorised dealers for India's top instrumentation brands.

  • Authorised dealers for 50+ leading industrial brands including Grundfos, Endress+Hauser, ABB, and more
  • End-to-end project execution — from design and supply to installation, commissioning and AMC
  • Certified technical team with 18+ years of domain expertise in process automation
  • Serving 1200+ clients across Maharashtra, Goa, Madhya Pradesh and across India

What We Offer

Our Core Services

Comprehensive industrial solutions from supply and installation to automation and after-sales support.

Pump Systems

Supply, installation and maintenance of centrifugal pumps, gear pumps, diaphragm pumps, peristaltic pumps and submersible pump systems for all industries.

Valves & Actuation

Complete range of ball valves, butterfly valves, globe valves, control valves, solenoid valves with manual, pneumatic and electric actuators.

Process Instrumentation

Supply and calibration of flow meters, pressure transmitters, temperature sensors, level instruments, pH & conductivity meters for process monitoring.

PLC / SCADA Automation

Design and commissioning of PLC-based control panels, SCADA systems, HMI configurations and full process automation for industrial plants.

AMC & Maintenance

Annual Maintenance Contracts ensuring uptime, preventive maintenance schedules, emergency breakdown support and spare parts availability.

Engineering Consultation

Expert engineering consultation for process design, equipment selection, piping layouts, energy optimisation and plant efficiency improvement projects.
