Our Story

Engineering Excellence Since 2005

Founded in 2005, Fluid-Tech began as a small trading firm for industrial pumps in Pune's Bhosari MIDC industrial zone. Over the years, fuelled by a passion for engineering and a relentless commitment to quality, the company expanded its portfolio to cover the entire fluid and process automation spectrum.

Today, Fluid-Tech is recognised as one of Pune's most trusted authorised dealers and system integrators for industrial instrumentation, pumping systems, valves, and automation. Our clients include leading names in pharmaceuticals, chemicals, food processing, water treatment, and manufacturing sectors across Maharashtra, Goa, and Madhya Pradesh.

We are proud to be the authorised dealer for global brands including Grundfos, Endress+Hauser, ABB, Bürkert, WIKA, Krohne, and many more — ensuring every customer receives genuine, certified, and warranty-backed products.

Our Journey

Growth Milestones

From a single-product trading firm to a multi-brand, multi-product system integrator — Fluid-Tech's journey reflects relentless ambition and a deep commitment to serving India's industrial sector.

2005
Company Founded

Fluid-Tech established in Pune's Bhosari MIDC as a pump and valve trading firm, serving local manufacturing units.

2008
First Authorised Dealership

Secured authorised dealership for a leading European pump brand, expanding product quality and market reach.

2011
Instrumentation Division Launch

Launched dedicated process instrumentation division — flow meters, transmitters, and level sensors added to portfolio.

2015
Automation & System Integration

Entered PLC/SCADA automation space, offering complete turnkey process automation systems and panel fabrication.

2018

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification, formally recognising Fluid-Tech's quality management systems and processes.

2022

Surpassed 1000 active clients across 6 states — a testament to quality, service, and long-term customer relationships.

2024
