Introduction

Technological Advancement & Innovation

We would like to introduce ourselves as Eminent Designers and Manufacturers of a wide spectrum of Flow Measurement (Flowmeters / Flow Sensors) and Dispensing Systems. Our single-minded focus towards Technological Advancement and Continuous Innovation and Research has enabled us to approach you to become a Privileged OEM supplier to your Organization.

We are well endowed with a Designing, Engineering, and Fabrication Division in our unit. We also would like to introduce ourselves as a Designer & Manufacturer of Systems like P.D. Flowmeters / Flow sensors, Automated Dispensing Systems, Oil Filtration Systems (NAS), Batching Systems, and Tanker Decanting System.

We are aware that nowadays most reputed organizations like you are carrying out Audits to keep track on Fuel Consumption in your organization. We take this opportunity to approach you to satisfy the needs with our Products - Fuel Consumption Monitoring System for Generators & Fleet/Vehicle.
